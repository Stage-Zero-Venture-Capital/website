Our Vision

We fund only the earliest — pre-revenue, pre-seed, pre-traction — and only when founders are ready to be sharpened, not just funded.

We Invest at Day Zero

Stage Zero VC backs startups before the world believes — before revenue, before users, and sometimes before product. If you're at Day 0 with bold conviction and raw ambition, we’ll help you shape that into a company worth building.

We Don't Fund Passengers

We’re not interested in anything beyond pre-seed. We say no to polish, ego, and safe ideas. We say yes to resilience, vision, and founders willing to be challenged. Our capital comes with advice — and the two are inseparable.

We Fix What’s Broken

The startup ecosystem in Australia is shy, underbuilt, and often visionless. Too many chase funding without clarity or courage. We’re here to flip that — by helping founders develop real vision and execute with focus.

We Shape Founders

You don’t pitch us a fairytale — you bring us your willingness to build something meaningful. If you accept our challenge, we’ll help you turn zero into win. But only if you commit.
