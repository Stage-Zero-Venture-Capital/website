Our Vision

We fund the absolute earliest — idea-stage, pre-revenue, pre-launch — and partner with founders who are ready to build fast, test often, and launch fearlessly.

We Invest at Day Zero

Stage Zero VC invests when it’s just you, a bold idea, and a dream worth chasing. We help you shape that into an MVP, get it in front of users, and build your early traction. No noise. Just execution.

We Don't Fund Passengers

We don’t do ego, pitch theatre, or over-polished decks. We back gritty builders solving real problems. Our capital comes with hard questions, honest feedback, and weekly hands-on support. That’s the deal.

We Fix What’s Broken

Most early-stage support in Australia is too slow, too vague, or too late. We show up when you’re still in the garage and help you ship, test, and iterate until you get your first users — and your first win.

Our Process

We always start with advice. Capital follows only when the partnership works.

1. Advisory First

We join your advisory board (or stay independent) to understand the team, tech, and trajectory—long before we talk term sheets.

2. Build the Relationship

Weekly sessions, brutal honesty, clear KPIs. We give advice and watch how quickly it turns into action.

3. Earn the Cheque

If the guidance helps and you execute relentlessly, we invest. No follow-through? No funding. Simple.
